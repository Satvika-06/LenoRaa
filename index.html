Why Choose Leno-Raa?

At Leno-Raa, we believe skincare should be backed by science and nature. Our soaps are handcrafted in small batches using the cold process method, allowing rich natural glycerine and organic oils to remain in every bar to nourish your skin.

  • Handmade Cold Process Soaps

    Cured for 4-6 weeks to retain structural integrity and moisturizing properties.

  • Doctor Formulated

    Created by dermatologists using scientifically researched botanical ingredients.

  • Skin Friendly

    pH balanced and highly nourishing for sensitive, dry, or irritated skin.

  • No Harsh Chemicals

    Free from parabens, sulfates, synthetic foaming agents, and mineral oils.

  • Rich Natural Oils

    Infused with extra virgin olive oil, organic coconut oil, and nourishing butter.

  • Suitable for Daily Use

    Gentle cleansing properties that preserve your skin's natural moisture barrier.

Cold process soapmaking is a traditional technique combining natural oils and sodium hydroxide (lye) without external heat. This preserves the natural glycerin (a powerful humectant) and botanical benefits of our ingredients, resulting in an exceptionally gentle, skin-nourishing bar.

Yes, absolutely. Because our soaps are formulated by dermatologists, we exclude sulfates, parabens, and synthetic foaming agents that trigger eczema, itching, or redness. Our Oatmeal, Goat Milk, and Aloe Vera soaps are highly recommended for ultra-sensitive skin.

Typically, a single Leno-Raa soap bar will last 3 to 4 weeks with daily usage. To maximize your bar's lifespan, keep it on a well-draining soap dish and let it dry fully between uses. Since we don't use chemical hardeners, keeping them away from standing water is key.
